About Us

We are one of the most trusted wholesalers and retailers of a wide range of Modular Kitchen Accessories, Kitchen Appliances, Exclusive Furniture Handles, Door Pull Handles, Mortise Handles, Sliding Solutions for Glass & Wooden Doors, Movable Walls, Modular Shop Fitting, Bathroom Accessories and all hardware solution for your home and business need. Our hardware fittings are known for Durability, Dimensional Accuracy and Fine Finish.1

We are Wholesalers and Retailers of Kitchen Hoods/Hobs, Square Kitchen Chimneys, Classic Ivory Brackets, Antique Mortice Handles.

We have been established in the year 1966, we, “Vasu Locks & Hardware Pvt Ltd”, are one of the most reputed wholesalers and retailers of a wide assortment of hardware,built in applinces for kitchen and shop fiiting solution. We provide the products are easy to install, light weight and attractive in design. We also offer the hardware fittings to clients in different sizes, shapes and colors, which meet their specific requirements. Our organization is associated with renowned national and international companies such as Hefele, Blum, Nagold, Orchid, Zaha, Wholemark, Kesseboomer, Godrej, Labacha, Dorma, Doorset, IPSA, Kent, Europa, Hardwyn, Yale, Ozone and many more from which we procure the products.

We have a wide spread network spread across Madhya Pradesh, which helps us in making products available to the clients. Our customers can avail different types of hardware fittings from us in required quantity. Presently, we are catering to the requirements of many commercial and corporate establishments. We are associated with renowned clients such as Hardwyn, Hettich, Kent, Ipsa and Ozone.

Our mentor, “Mr. Vasu Locks”, is a man of vast industry experience and sharp business acumen. He has motivated us to carry out our business practices on the basis of ethical grounds. Owing to his support, we have scaled new heights.

Quality Assurance

Quality is the basic motive of our organization and we take maximum care to maintain the standard of our products, so that the existing market reputation remain intact. Our range of Kitchen Fittings, Curtain Brackets, Handles and Drawer Slides is procured from reputed organizations such as Ebco, Salice, Zipco, Kitchen and Jewel.These organizations use superior quality raw material to manufacture the hardware fittings.
